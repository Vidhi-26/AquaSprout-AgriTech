Building a sustainable future with Agri-Tech
A hydroponics set-up with IoT & mobile integration to produce quality vegetables & fruits, best for your health

The Problem

  • Global warming makes farming difficult in an uncontrolled environment
  • This compromises quality of vegetables and fruits
  • Due to COVID-19, inconsistent supply chains worsen the situation
  • According to USA Today, an apple bought from a supermarket, on average, is one year old
  • Harmful products for consumers like you

The Solution

  • An agri-tech revolution with hydroponics and aquaponics technology
  • Products grown with Controlled Environment Agriculture (CEA) technique
  • 100% residue free vegetables, herbs, and fruits
  • Perennial availibility and consisten quality of fruits and vegetables
  • Safe products you can trust

IoT-based quality control

At Flourish AgriTech, we leverage IoT technology to automate quality control. Sensors for temperature, light, water flow, pH level, etc. are powered by IoT. Continuous monitoring of the growth of veggies and fruits through these sensors allows production of best quality produce.

Mobile integration

Download our app and sign up to get alerts when an inconsistency in temperature, pH level, water level, etc. is detected by our sensors. Our app also shows detailed stats, charts, and graphs to monitor the daily growth of vegetables and fruits. Gone are the days of worrying, be assured with Flourish AgriTech!
